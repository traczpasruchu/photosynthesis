How do plants get their initial energy?

Light from the sun serves a very important role to a plant, and, by extension, to us and every other animal on the planet. The sun provides the essential energy needed to power photosynthesis. Without photosynthesis, we wouldn't have oxygen. You can probably imagine what would happen then. The leaf absorbs energy from the sun to power all of the processes involved in photosynthesis
